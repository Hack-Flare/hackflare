# Getting Started

Welcome to HackFlare! This guide will walk you through setting up your account and managing your first domain.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit the [HackFlare dashboard](/dash) and click "Sign in"
2. Register with your email or use your Hack Club account
3. Verify your email address to activate your account

## Adding Your First Domain

Once you're logged in:

1. Navigate to the **Domains** section in the dashboard
2. Click **Add Domain** and enter your domain name
3. Copy the nameservers provided
4. Update your domain registrar's nameservers to point to HackFlare

## Verifying Your Domain

After updating nameservers, HackFlare will automatically verify ownership. This can take a few minutes depending on DNS propagation. We recommend manually clicking the **Verify** button in the dashboard to speed up the process.

You'll see your domain status change from "Pending" to "Active" once verified.
